About Revolt

Steve Mcfarland, Gallery Owner & Curator

McFarland - who fell in love with art in high school where he says he seemed to fail everything else -- helped tear down venue walls or find food and sound for parties. With Revolt, McFarland stepped into full-on leadership roles -- contacting artists, creating flyers, organizing catering - host from beginning to end. (Taos News, Arielle Christian)

Since our opening in 2017, Taos Revolt Gallery has sought to offer a space for local, emerging, and underground artists to be heard in the TAOS community.

Taos Revolt has transformed its historic downtown site into a dynamic, thriving facility for artistic exploration. We enjoy hosting a variety of events and exhibits - from small to large scale installations, immersive music, art, performances, and more.

"This is all for the community and to fill the void in this art town that doesn't really have events for our age group," Steve McFarland, (Taos News, Arielle Christian)

Sarah Stoler, Co-curator

Sarah Stolar

Sarah Stolar is an interdisciplinary artist living in Santa Fe, New Mexico. The breadth of her work includes painting, drawing, multimedia installation, film, video, and performance art. Sarah, an internationally award-winning filmmaker, including Best Experimental Film at Synergy Film Festival, New York City and finalist for the Andy Warhol Award for Best Experimental Film in the US. A committed educator for over fifteen years, Sarah Stolar is currently the Chair of the Department of Fine Arts, Film and Digital Media at the University of New Mexico - Taos.

As of 2020, Taos Revolt Gallery renewed its mission to support artists from diverse backgrounds and elevate the voices of Black, Indigenous, People of Color, and LGBTQIA+ people. We seek to build equality through a strong creative community, inclusive of all groups and individuals.

Taos Revolt Gallery is committed to artistic and creative freedom for all.

Come view the art at the gallery, listen to live music, enjoy local food, and join us in building this community.
