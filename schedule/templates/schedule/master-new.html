REVOLT

Since our opening in 2017, Taos Revolt Gallery has sought to offer a space for local, emerging, and underground artists to be heard in the TAOS community. We offer a reflection of New Mexico's unique and evolving cultural landscape through Taos' growing local talent, alongside a number of international artists who share our vision. As of 2020, Taos Revolt Gallery renewed its mission to support artists from diverse backgrounds and elevate the voices of Black, Indigenous, People of Color, and LGBTQIA+ people. We seek to build equality through a strong, inclusive and creative community. Taos Revolt Gallery is committed to artistic and creative freedom for all.

Formerly the location of the Hulse-Warman Gallery, Taos Revolt has transformed its historic downtown site into a dynamic, thriving facility for artistic exploration. With unique indoor and outdoor areas, we enjoy hosting a variety of events and exhibits - from small to large scale installations, immersive music, art, performances, and more. Taos Revolt is thrilled to present an ever-changing rotation of exhibits. Come view the art at the gallery, listen to live music, enjoy local food, and be with community.
