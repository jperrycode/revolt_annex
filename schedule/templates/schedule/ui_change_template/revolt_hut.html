The Hut Out Front

Revolt & Arroyo Seco Live

‘The Hut’ Artist Open Call

Revolt Gallery and Arroyo Seco Live are inviting Northern New Mexico regional artists to apply in our ‘Open Call’ for installations in Taos downtown main street-facing’ art space in front of Revolt Gallery, ‘The Hut’: taking applications now for month-long displays from May 2024 through December 2024.

Artists are given wide creative freedom to address the 2024 theme of ‘Reflection’. Artists may address the theme in a conceptual sense (“This is what I am thinking about; this is what we should be thinking about; this is the result of thinking about certain topic/topics) or in a literal sense (This is a reflective element composed in installation) or a combination of approaches: the main point being, the creative terms are open.

Artists interested in doing performance/in-person activity in the space are acceptable with respect to scheduling.

DATES: Applications open now for displays taking place each month starting May 1st, 2024, through December 1st, 2024.

“Vulnerability” by Laura Feierman

Mini Installation

Living and practicing in New Mexico, Laura Feierman is an interdisciplinary artist that integrates beauty, identity, and personal experiences into 3-dimensional sculpture installments.

She is a Summa Cum Laude graduate from the University of New Mexico with a BFA in Studio Art/Sculpture.

Her Wo/Manhouse 2022 installation in the pink bathroom combines her personal experiences with vulnerability, insecurity, humility, and tenacity while maintaining femininity, fantasy, and female empowerment.
