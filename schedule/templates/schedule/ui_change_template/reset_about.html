Steve Mcfarland, Gallery Owner & Curator

The Annex space, which opened in December 2022, is the latest expression of McFarland’s vision for an artistically vital north side.
Richard Aufrichtig Apr 3, 2023 Updated Apr 6, 2023

Alongside the ongoing roller skating classes held on Mondays and various community events throughout the week in the performing arts space in the back, the much-anticipated Godega from LeeCooks Church and Mark Brumbelo’s Heads Up Music record store have found their footing and are thriving since their opening.

These businesses have become integral parts of the community, adding vibrancy and diversity to the area. Additionally, the top floor of the building continues to serve as a residency space for visiting artists, while the bottom floor remains home to three studio spaces allocated for locals.
