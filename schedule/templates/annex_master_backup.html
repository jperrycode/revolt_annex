About Revolt Gallery

Gallery and Annex

Revolt Gallery and Reset Music Space offers an incredibly vast offering of different arts to help free your mind from the norm. Owner and operator, Steve McFarland opens his instalation filled property to any passer by. On the weekends, the venue, Reset hosts a wide array of different bands and DJ's to stimulate the mind.
